Epigraph

In fancy they pursue
The dream-child moving through a land
Of wonders wild and new,
In friendly chat with bird or beast--
And half believe it true."

Preface

This emoji translation was a difficult process. Some words don't have an emoji to match with and some words don't match perfectly with the emoji's, but I had to try my best. Some words were paired with a very literal emoji, such as the word "I" was paired with an eye emoji. Some emoji's stand for the same word, such as "think" and "wonder."
